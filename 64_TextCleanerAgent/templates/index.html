About 100DaysOfAI-Agents Challenge

The 100DaysOfAI-Agents challenge is a journey to build 100 unique, creative, and useful AI-powered agents in 100 days. Each day brings a new project, new learning, and a new opportunity to push the boundaries of what AI can do for real-world problems and creative expression.

  • Daily hands-on coding and design
  • Focus on practical, inspiring, and impactful AI agents
  • Open source, community-driven, and collaborative
  • Showcases the power of modern AI APIs and UI/UX
